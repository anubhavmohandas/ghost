/ powers

Four ways
to haunt
a form.

Click once, fill everything. Or only the empties. Or just hover a single field and let the ghost ask politely. Or skip the form entirely and inject your test payload into whatever field is focused. It's your form. It's your call.

P / 01
Haunt All.

One click. Every detected field. Filled instantly across name, address, KYC, banking, social, login — the whole form, before your coffee gets cold.

⚡
P / 02
Smart Fill.

Polite mode. Fills only the empty fields and leaves whatever you've already typed alone. Perfect for the half-finished forms life keeps giving you.

◐
P / 03
Hover Pill.

Hover any input. A little ghost appears: "May I?" Click yes. Field filled. When the field has no match, it pouts. We've all been there.

👻
P / 04
Payloads.

For the security crowd. Save named test strings and inject them into the focused field with one tap. CTF, bug bounty, repeat input — your kind of evening.

/ security

It stays
on your device.

GHOST is the rare productivity tool that doesn't phone home. Zero analytics. Zero external requests. Everything lives in chrome.storage.local, optionally encrypted with a PIN, optionally exported with a passphrase.

The v5.5 release added auto-lock, IRCTC train booking fill, and three new themes — on top of the v5.4 at-rest encryption with two-key architecture that security auditors like to see.

Zero telemetry Local only Open source
SEC / 01

PIN-Lock & at-rest encryption.

Two PBKDF2 derivations from your PIN — one verification hash (stored), one AES-GCM key (held in popup memory only, cleared on close). Sensitive fields — credentials, cards, bank, GSTIN — encrypted before they touch disk.

SEC / 02

Export passphrase.

Backups are AES-GCM 256-bit encrypted with a PBKDF2-derived key. Same 600k iterations. Same 32-byte salt. No window.prompt() — a real inline modal with show/hide. Or skip the passphrase and get plain JSON, your call.

SEC / 03

Message-sender verification.

The background service worker checks sender.id === chrome.runtime.id on every incoming message. External scripts pretending to be the extension get nothing. Hover-pill rect-guards prevent layout-ghost off-screen draws.

/ themes

Pick a haunt.
Re-skin everything.

Four built-in themes. Click any swatch and the entire page — header, gradients, orbs, buttons, popup, the works — shifts in real time. The custom-builder inside the extension lets you go further with ten live color pickers. We won't judge your taste.

/ install

One extension.

Manifest V3 for Chrome, Edge, and Brave. A signed MV2 build for Firefox. A short Xcode dance for Safari. Then it's Cmd+Shift+F from anywhere. Tag a release, GitHub Actions ships all three zips. Run update.sh to keep current.
