## Full description
GHOST — General Handler of Stored Templates

Most autofill tools only cover name and email. GHOST fills everything.

79 field types detected automatically across every kind of form:
— Job applications (CTC, notice period, skills, experience, education)
— KYC & banking (Aadhaar, PAN, passport, IFSC, card details)
— Government portals (voter ID, driving licence, nationality)
— Social & contact (LinkedIn, GitHub, Twitter, WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord)
— Security testing (custom payloads injected into focused fields)
— Standard forms (name, email, phone, address, login credentials)

KEY FEATURES

✦ Smart detection — matches fields by name, id, autocomplete, placeholder, label, aria-label
✦ Haunt All — fill every detected field in one click
✦ Smart Fill — fill only empty fields, leave pre-filled ones untouched
✦ Hover pill — hover over any field and see "👻 May I?" — click to fill just that field
✦ Per-site profile binding — GHOST auto-selects your saved profile for that site
✦ Dictation mode — speak your details, GHOST types them in
✦ Multiple profiles — work, personal, testing — switch instantly
✦ AES-GCM 256-bit encrypted export/import — your data never leaves your device
✦ Dark & light theme
✦ Keyboard shortcut — Cmd+Shift+F (Mac) / Ctrl+Shift+F (Windows/Linux)

PRIVACY
All data stored locally via chrome.storage.local. Zero external requests. Zero analytics.
